About Us

Air German is a Virtual Airline based in Microsoft Flight Simulator 2020, Microsoft Flight Simulator 2024 and X-Plane 12. It got founded on the 22th of May, 2024 by Noah. Air German is based in Berlin with hubs in Munich and Frankfurt am Main. As a Flight Tracking Software we are using Newsky.

Our Mission

We connect pilots from all over the world, creating a realistic and enternaining virtual flight experience. Together we take off!

Our Route Network

Fly over 100 realistic routes, carefully planned by experienced flight enthusiasts. From short-haul to long-haul flights - there's something for everyone.

Pilot Training Academy

New to flying? No worries! Our Pilot Training Academy guides you step by step to ensure safe flights and maximum enjoyment with your simulator.
