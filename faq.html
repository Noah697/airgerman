Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I join Air German as a pilot?
You can apply to become a pilot on our Discord by opening a ticket. Once accepted, you will receive all necessary instructions and access to our systems.
What flight simulator do I need to fly for Air German?
We support multiple flight simulators, including Microsoft Flight Simulator (MSFS) and X-Plane. You must also use our ACARS system to log your flights (newsky).
I’m having trouble installing the Air German Virtual live tracker. What should I do?
If you have problems with the installation, you can ask other community members or pilots for help. If you still have problems, you can contact support in the #general-support channel.
Do I need to complete a minimum number of flights per month?
Yes, to maintain an active status in our airline, pilots must complete at least 3 flights per month. If you are unable to fly for a while, please inform us so we can keep your account active.
Is there an age requirement to join?
Yes, you must be at least 13 years old to join Air German Virtual. This complies with Discord’s and other platform policies.
