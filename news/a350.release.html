We are excited to announce the introduction of the Airbus A350 to our fleet! This milestone marks a significant expansion as we welcome the highly anticipated iniBuilds A350 – the first A350 available for Microsoft Flight Simulator (MSFS).

Initially, the A350 will be available exclusively for MSFS 2020. We anticipate making it accessible for MSFS 2024 in the coming weeks.

Our new A350 fleet consists of the following aircraft:

  • D-AOIU "München" – Airbus A350-900, stationed in Munich (EDDM)
  • D-AOIV "Frankfurt" – Airbus A350-900, stationed in Frankfurt (EDDF)
  • D-AOIW "Berlin" – Airbus A350-1000, stationed in Berlin (EDDB)

Today, we will be flying these new aircraft from Toulouse (LFBO) to their new homes in Germany. This special delivery flight marks the beginning of a new chapter for Air German Virtual – and we are excited to share this journey with you!

The Airbus A350 is one of the most advanced and fuel-efficient aircraft in the world. Its innovative design and lightweight composite materials allow it to consume up to 25% less fuel compared to older aircraft models. With its ultra-quiet Rolls-Royce Trent XWB engines and state-of-the-art aerodynamics, the A350 offers pilots an unmatched flying experience. Its advanced cockpit systems, enhanced flight controls, and cutting-edge avionics make it a dream to operate.

If you would like to obtain a type rating for the Airbus A350 and be part of this exciting journey, please open a ticket on our Discord server and our team will guide you through the process.

Welcome aboard our new A350s – and as always, Happy Landings!
